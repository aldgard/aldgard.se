Travel

Whistler

Canada

Info

Whistler resort is rightly considered to be one of the hottest ski resorts in the world. Whistler is located in the Costal Mountains mountain range in a very beautiful setting. The town has a long history dating back to 1877 when the railroad was merged there. The city has also hosted the 2010 Olympic Winter Games.

  • Total Slope Length: 216 km
  • Longest Slope: 11 km
  • Transfer time: 120 min
  • Highets Peak: 2284 m.a.s.l
  • Number of lifts: 33

Davos

Switzerland

Info

Davos is Europe's highest city and has everything one could wish for from a ski resort. The 5 different lifts that you easily get between form a superb ski area with a wide range of slopes and fantastic possibilities for off-piste skiing.

  • Total Slope Length: 310 km
  • Longest Slope: 12 km
  • Transfer time: 150 min
  • Highets Peak: 2844 m.a.s.l
  • Number of lifts: 49

St Anton

Austria

Info

In St. Anton, one of the absolute best skiing systems in the Alps is one of Europe's most snow-capped resorts. St. Anton is a real good ski resort with a cruel offpiste, a large and varied piste system and many cozy restaurants and bars. If you are looking for a party there is of course a really good entertainment life. St. Anton is the perfect ski resort for boys or girls where you combine cruel skiing with the Alps' best après ski. But St Anton is also suitable for families and conference trips.

  • Total Slope Length: 280 km
  • Longest Slope: 10,8 km
  • Transfer time: 150 min
  • Highets Peak: 2811 m.a.s.l
  • Number of lifts: 37
Trail map

Zermatt

Switzerland

Info

At the foot of the Toblerone mountain Matterhorn is one of Europe's best ski areas, Zermatt / Cervinia a total of 350 km of piste and something for everyone. Wide slopes, most parks, puckles and controlled offpiste. Outside the piste there is a lot to choose from, slopes in all directions mean that they can often find untouched snow. Zermatt's most spectacular off-piste yard is Schwarztor, after rounding Breithorn awaits a long offpiste ride over Schwartzglacier down to Furi. The view from the ski area is fantastic, nowhere else in the Alps can you see as many peaks over 4,000 meters at the same time.

  • Total Slope Length: 350 km
  • Longest Slope: 20 km
  • Transfer time: 220 min
  • Highets Peak: 3883 m.a.s.l
  • Number of lifts: 54
